03 / Research

Papers &
publications.

Paper 01

Chunking Is the Real Bottleneck in RAG

Co-authored with Dr. Arockiaraj S · MSIS, MAHE Manipal

A systematic, chunking-centric study of RAG pipelines isolating document segmentation as the single variable. Evaluated multiple strategies across retrieval effectiveness, context quality, faithfulness, and hallucination behavior — establishing chunking as a first-order design decision rather than a preprocessing detail.

Under Review Nov – Dec 2025
Paper 02

Deep Learning Models for Generalized Cancer Detection from Histopathology Images

Co-authored with Dr. Keerthana Prasad · MSIS, MAHE Manipal

Generalized DL framework for cancer detection across multiple histopathology datasets, addressing domain shift from staining/scanner variations. Evaluated transformer- and CNN-based architectures including ViT-B/16 under cross-domain generalization conditions.

Under Review Aug – Dec 2025
Paper 03

Systematic Analysis of Batch Reactor Thermal Dynamics Through Visual Analytics

Co-authored with Dr. Arockiaraj S · MSIS, MAHE Manipal

Visualization-driven analysis of open-loop batch reactor dynamics using 7,201-sample dataset over 3,600s. Applied 14 analytical techniques including PCA, clustering, phase-space visualization, and frequency-domain analysis to identify asymmetric thermal transport delays and dominant system time constants.

Under Review Nov – Dec 2025
Paper 04

Fake News Classification for a Regional Language

Co-authored with Dr. Narendra V G · MIT Manipal

Multilingual fake news classification pipeline using contextual text representations and classical ML. Curated regional-language news dataset with rule-based filtering, normalization, and deduplication. Built interactive interface for real-time model comparison.

Under Review Jan – May 2025
